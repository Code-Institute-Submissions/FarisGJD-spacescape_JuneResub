Assignment Expedition

The year is 2083; you are stationed 1402 light years away, by the exoplanet Kepler-452b otherwise known as Earth 2.0. A coalition of world leaders and government bodies have assigned you the mission of exploring the potential habitability of Kepler which is thought to be desolate.

As you exist your spacecraft and approach Kepler’s atmosphere, you notice a strange grouping of objects hurdling towards you. OH NO! It looks like the native species have detected your presence and are not too pleased. Quick; avoid being captured or worse killed and get back to your spaceship, reporting your findings to ground control.

Manoeuvers

The strategy is simple, avoid the obstacles by clicking the ‘Space’ key or double tap on mobile/tablet screens to make your sprite jump, avoiding death. Time your movements wisely, the longer you’re outside the safety of your ship the harder the obstacles become. Good Luck!
